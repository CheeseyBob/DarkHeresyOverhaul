Talents - Dark Heresy Overhaul Index

Talents

Talents are a particular kind of Aspect which can be permenantly attached to characters. They cannot be Overcome and they each have one of the following effects on the character with the Talent:

  • Grant a new action.
  • Enhance the effects of an action.
  • Give a +10 bonus to an Attack action in a certain circumstance (bonuses to other types of Action are covered by Skills).

Talent List

  • Brutal Charge: Charge with double the bonus damage.
  • Crushing Blow: Heavy Blow with double the bonus damage.
  • Strong Attacks: Bulky Melee Weapons use double your Strength Bonus for damage.
  • Sweeping Blow: Melee Attack (maximum hits 1) with an extra hit on a different target for each Degree of Success.
  • Fearless: Immune to Pinning and Fear.
  • [Melee Weapon] Mastery: Get a +10 bonus to Melee Attack with [Melee Weapon]
  • [Ranged Weapon] Mastery: Get a +10 bonus to Ranged Attack with [Ranged Weapon]
  • Wrestler: Get a +10 bonus to Grapple Attack.
  • Gunslinger: Get a +10 bonus to Ranged Attack when using two pistols.
  • Dual-Wielder: Get a +10 bonus to Melee Attack when using two weapons.
  • Furious Assault: Get a +10 bonus to Flurry of Blows when using two weapons.
  • Lightning Attack: Get a +10 bonus to Flurry of Blows.
  • Quick Dagger: Small bladed weapons get (+1|+2) hits.
  • Quick Blade: Standard bladed weapons get (+0|+1) hits.
  • Sprinter: Your maximum bonus Movement is three times your base Movement, rather than two times.
  • Quick Draw: Draw your weapon without spending an Action.
  • Leap Up: Stand without spending an Action.
  • Precision Strike: With a Small bladed weapon, Melee Attack with maximum hits reduced to 1 and a +1 bonus to damage for each Degree of Success.
  • Careful Shot: Get an extra +10 when applying a bonus from Aim.
  • Careful Strike: Get an extra +10 when applying a bonus from Bide.
  • Careful Aim: Get an extra +10 bonus from Extended Aim.
  • Sniper Aim: Bonuses from Aim persist if you take another Aim action.
  • Rapid Reload: Reload as a Secondary Action.
  • Sound Constitution: Get an extra Wound.
  • Pirouette: Spend a Degree of Success on a Dodge to not spend your Reaction.
  • Wall of Steel: Spend a Degree of Success on a Parry to not spend your Reaction.
  • Agile Dodge: Spend Degrees of Success on a Dodge to move 1m per DoS, up to a maximum of your Movement.
  • Counter-Attack: Spend Degrees of Success on a Parry to get a +10 bonus per DoS to Melee Attack.
  • Follow-Through: Spend Degrees of Success on a Melee Attack to get a +10 bonus per DoS to Melee Attack.
  • Assault Fire: Move twice and Ranged Attack during the movement.
  • Assassin Strike: Move twice and Melee Attack during the movement.
  • Assassin Roll: You can Move unimpeeded when Prone.
  • Independent Targeting: When wielding two ranged weapons, you can Ranged Attack against two targets, assigning hits from one weapon to the second target.
  • Fast Retargeting: You can Extended Fire against two targets, assigning each hit to either of the targets.
