Rank Descriptions

    • Archivists: Tend to the needs of those higher up the ranks.
    • Scriveners: Deal with scrolls, books and data-slates.
    • Scribes: Collect and extract information.
    • Auditors: Conducts audits to record data and ensure regulations are followed.
    • Medicators: Provide medical services.
    • Scholars: Study their field in depth and sometimes provide expert advice to those in positions of power.
    • Operators: Operate the machines and vehicles within the Imperium's civilian infrastructure.
    • Chirurgeons: Perform advanced medical procedures.
    • Lexographers: Study multiple fields and the connections between them.
    • Comptrollers: Direct the flow of information as it passes through the Administratum, and manage its most complicated systems.
    • Master Chirurgeons: Top medical practitioners who are often employed by the nobility.
    • Loremasters: Study deeper mysteries and knowledge that has been hidden or forgotten.
    • Logisters: Apply their phenomenal powers of reasoning to the toughest problems the Administratum has to deal with.
    • Sage Loremasters: Delve into the deepest secrets and mysteries of the galaxy.
    • Sage Logisters: Apply their phenomenal powers of reasoning to the toughest problems the Administratum has to deal with.
