Character Creation

1. Roll Up The Character

After rolling a character, you can Burn a Fate Point to bench that character and roll a new one.

  1. Roll a Home World using the table below.
  2. Roll a Background using the table below.
  3. Roll a Role using the table below.
  4. Roll 2d10 + 20 for each Characteristic. For Characteristics in the bonus column for the Home World, reroll and keep the highest. For Characteristics in the penalty column, reroll and keep the lowest.
  5. Set your Maximum Wounds to 5 + 2x Toughness Bonus.

2. Home World Benefits

  1. Pick one of the two Characteristics in the bonus column for your homeworld to take as an Aptitude.
  2. Pick one Talent listed under your Home World.
Feral World
Abhuman: You belong to one of the standard, stable morphological types of mutant tolerated by the authorities (roll on the Mutations table).
Natural Expertise (Beasts): For this area, you can Reference Common Lore instead of Reference Scholastic Lore.
Resistance (Cold/Heat/Poison): Get a +20 bonus to Resist this.
Tracker: Get a +20 bonus to Search when following tracks.
Trade (Apothecary): Blend and prepare herbal remedies. You can Craft and Repair and get a +20 bonus to other Tests when relevant.
Trade (Soothsayer): Give the appearance of telling the future. You can Craft and Repair and get a +20 bonus to other Tests when relevant.
Trade (Survivalist): Scavenge and create primitive items from scavenged materials. You can Craft and Repair and get a +20 bonus to other Tests when relevant.
Forge World
Knowledgable (Tech): Get a +20 bonus to Reference Common Lore for this area.
Bionic Arm: Functions as a regular arm. You do not become Stunned or Bleeding Out from critical damage to this arm.
Bionic Eye: Functions as a regular eye. You take at most a -10 penalty from being Dazzled.
Bionic Leg: Functions as a regular leg. You do not become Stunned or Bleeding Out from critical damage to this leg.
Bionic Lungs: Functions as a pair of lungs. Get a +20 bonus to Resist harmful gases.
Interface Port: You can connect to data-slates, cogitators and other tech devices via a data cable. You get a +10 bonus to relevant Tests when doing so.
Vocal Implant: Functions as a voice, which you can amplify greatly. Get a +10 bonus to Intimidate.
Highborn
Educated (2 Areas): You can Reference Scholastic Lore for this area.
Slush Fund: On most populated worlds, you have a contact from whom you can Requisition funds.
Special Aquisitions: On most populated worlds, you have a contact from whom you can Requisition any item with an Availability up to Extremely Rare, though it may take them some time to get things to you.
Natural Expertise (Heraldry): For this area, you can Reference Common Lore instead of Reference Scholastic Lore.
Trade (Cook): Cook meals and inspect food. You can Craft and Repair and get a +20 bonus to other Tests when relevant.
Trade (Merchant): Find, bargain and sell trade goods. You can Craft and Repair and get a +20 bonus to other Tests when relevant.
Trade (Performer): Perform for an audience. You can Craft and Repair and get a +20 bonus to other Tests when relevant.
Trade (Valet): Refine the appearance, give droll asides and tend to the needs of superiors in a gentlemanly fashion. You can Craft and Repair and get a +20 bonus to other Tests when relevant.
Hive World
Accustomed to Darkness: You reduce penalties from Darkness by 30.
Caves of Metal: Get +20 to Navigate when in a hive, underground caves or the like.
Clues from the Crowds: Get a +20 bonus to Inquire when gathering information from a crowd of people.
Face in a Crowd: You can use Fellowship instead of Agility when you Sneak in a populated area.
Knowledgable (Tech): Get a +20 bonus to Reference Common Lore for this area.
Networking: Long Action; Fellowship Test; gain a trusted contact in this area; gain Attention on critical failure; you can maintain a number of contacts up to your Fellowship Bonus.
Trade (Cook): Cook meals and inspect food. You can Craft and Repair and get a +20 bonus to other Tests when relevant.
Shrine World
Knowledgable (Calixis Sector): Get a +20 bonus to Reference Common Lore for this area.
Knowledgable (Imperial Creed): Get a +20 bonus to Reference Common Lore for this area.
Public Speaker: When making a Fellowship Test targeting a group, instead of splitting Degrees of Success amongst the targets, all targets up to FBx10m away are affected as if they were the only target.
Trade (Cook): Cook meals and inspect food. You can Craft and Repair and get a +20 bonus to other Tests when relevant.
Trade (Scrimshaw): Inscribe patterns, text and art onto things. You can Craft and Repair and get a +20 bonus to other Tests when relevant.
Trade (Sculptor): Create inspiring works of art in stone, metal and other materials. You can Craft and Repair and get a +20 bonus to other Tests when relevant.
Tithe World
Operate (Wheeled/Tracked/Walker): You can operate this vehicle type using Intelligence.
Resistance (Cold/Heat/Poison): Get a +20 bonus to Resist this.
Natural Expertise (Beasts): For this area, you can Reference Common Lore instead of Reference Scholastic Lore.
Trade (Agri Worker): Grow, care for, and harvest crops and animals. You can Craft and Repair and get a +20 bonus to other Tests when relevant.
Trade (Cook): Cook meals and inspect food. You can Craft and Repair and get a +20 bonus to other Tests when relevant.
Trade (Miner): Extract minerals, maintain mines and identify mining hazards. You can Craft and Repair and get a +20 bonus to other Tests when relevant.
Trade (Prospector): Find and identify valuable minerals. You can Craft and Repair and get a +20 bonus to other Tests when relevant.
Void Born
Accustomed to Darkness: You reduce penalties from Darkness by 30.
Accustomed to Zero Gravity: You reduce penalties from Zero Gravity by 30.
Knowledgable (Tech): Get a +20 bonus to Reference Common Lore for this area.
Natural Expertise (Astromancy): For this area, you can Reference Common Lore instead of Reference Scholastic Lore.
Resistance (Vacuum): Get a +20 bonus to Resist this.
Strong Mind: Get a +20 bonus to Resist (Mentally) against Psychic Powers.
Touched By The Warp: You can Sense Immaterium even if you are not a psyker.
Trade (Voidfarer): The day-to-day operation, logistics and defence of starships. You can Craft and Repair and get a +20 bonus to other Tests when relevant.
War World
First Aider: Get a +20 bonus to Treat Wounds when performing first aid.
Hard Target: When you are Running, you give a -20 penalty to all ranged attacks against you.
Knowledgable (War): Get a +20 bonus to Reference Common Lore for this area.
Nerves of Steel: Get a +20 bonus to Resist being Frightened, Intimidated and Pinned.
Sprinter: Your maximum bonus Movement is three times your Agility Bonus, rather than two times.
Trade (Survivalist): Scavenge and create primitive items from scavenged materials. You can Craft and Repair and get a +20 bonus to other Tests when relevant.

    5. Spend Experience and Equip Gear

    Each character should have 8 different Aptitudes, including General. If you took the same Aptitude more than once, you can choose any Characteristic as an Aptitude to make up for this.

    Equipment:

    • Take any Ubiquitous or Abundant items as you can want (and can carry).
    • You can swap out items from your background for different ones of the same availability.
    • You can also take any 1 Scarce item or 2 Average items.

    Experience:

    • You start with 10 XP.
    • Buy any Advances you wish with this XP (see Character Advancement). Keep any XP you don't spend.
