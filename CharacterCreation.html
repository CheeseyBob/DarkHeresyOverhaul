Character Creation - Dark Heresy Overhaul Index

Character Creation

1. Characteristics

Set your Fate Points to 3. Roll 2d10+20 for each Characteristic.

2. Home World

Roll on the table to determine the character's home world. Reroll each bonus Characteristic and take the highest result. Reroll each penalty Characteristic and take the lowest result. Finally, choose a Talent listed for your home world.
