Range: When you Attack, you get a bonus or penalty for range, according to the table on the weapon card. Use the highest bonus for which the target is within the corresponding distance (for example, you always get a +30 bonus to a target 3m away). Firing at a target beyond the maximum range is ineffective.

RoF (Rate of Fire): This is the maximum number of shots fired by the weapon in one turn. One shot is fired when you Attack, whereas Attack Repeatedly fires a number of shots equal to the weapon's RoF.

Capacity: How much ammo the weapon can hold.

Reload: How many Secondary Actions it takes to reload the weapon. You can also Reload as a Main Action to speed this up.

Damage: The damage the target takes from each hit. The Type determines how effective armour is and what effects there are if critical damage is inflicted.

Availability: The base availability for Ranged Weapons is Abundant.

Overheats
When this weapon jams, roll a d10:
1-5: The Aspect is upgraded to Jammed(20). You take 1d10 Energy Damage unless you choose to drop the weapon.
6-8: The Aspect is upgraded to Jammed(30). You take 1d10+2 Energy Damage and drop the weapon.
9-0: The weapon explodes and is destroyed. You and anyone within 4m takes a hit from the weapon.
