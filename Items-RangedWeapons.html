Range: The maximum effective range of the weapon. Apply a bonus/penalty for the range of the target based on the table below. If an adjacent target is helpless or unaware, use the 2m bonus instead of the CQC penalty.

Range Bonuses/Penalites (Ranged Weapons)
Range CQC 2m 5m 10m 20m 40m 80m 160m 320m 640m 1280m
Bonus -20 +30 +20 +10 +0 -10 -20 -30 -40 -50 -60
Apply an additional +30 if the target is static/helpless/unaware.

RoF (Rate of Fire): This is the maximum number of shots fired by the weapon in one turn. One shot is fired when you Attack, whereas Attack Repeatedly fires a number of shots equal to the weapon's RoF.

Capacity: How much ammo the weapon can hold.

Reload: How many Secondary Actions it takes to reload the weapon. You can also Reload as a Main Action to speed this up.

Damage: The damage the target takes from each hit. The Type determines how effective armour is and what effects there are if critical damage is inflicted.

Overcharge(3)
Before you Attack, you can spend up to 3 additional ammo. Doing so reduces the RoF to 1 for that attack and gives a 1d10 bonus to damage for each additional ammo spent. The additional ammo spent counts towards jamming.
Overheats
When this weapon jams, roll a d10:
1-5: The Jammed aspect is upgraded. You take 1d10 Energy damage unless you drop the weapon.
6-9: The weapon becomes Damaged. You take 1d10+2 Energy damage and drop the weapon.
10: The weapon explodes and is destroyed. You and anyone within 4m takes a hit from the weapon.

Special Rules
Name Av. Description
Belt-Fed +1 Ammo is not contained in the weapon, but drawn from a container which must be placed beside the weapon.
Electric Power(N) +0 This weapon uses N electric power per shot instead of consuming ammo.
Feed Line +0 Ammo is not contained in the weapon, but drawn from a container via a feed line.

Reliable -1 This weapon cannot become Jammed.
Overcharge(N) -2N+1 Before you Attack, you can spend up to N additional ammo. Doing so reduces the RoF to 1 for that attack and gives a 1d10 bonus to damage for each additional ammo spent. The additional ammo spent counts towards jamming.
Scoped -1 If you Aim as a Full Action, you ignore penalties from range.
Suppressing Fire -1 When you Attack Repeatedly with this weapon, you can spend your Reaction to fire twice as many shots and make all characters within 20m in a 45 degree arc become Pinned.
Ablative -2 Hits from this weapon damage cover, armour and other items.
Burning Wounds -2 Each hit that inflicts Wounds makes the target become Burning.
Scatter Shot -3 Within 10m, each hit counts as 2 hits. Within 5m each hit counts as 3 hits.
Burning Hits -3 Each hit makes the target become Burning.
Spray(N) -4-N Use double the range bonus/penalty. Maximum hits is N when you Attack or 2N if you Attack Repeatedly. Treat failure as a single hit. You can apply each hit to any target within a 45 degree arc.
*Weapons with Spray(N) should have RoF 2.

Notes

  • An extra hit / an extra d10 of damage is worth 2 availability.
  • For weapons using Electric Power(N), multiply the ammo capacity by N.
