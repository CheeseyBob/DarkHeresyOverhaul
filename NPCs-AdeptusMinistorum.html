Rank Descriptions

    • Novice: Serves those higher than themselves in the Ecclesiarchy.
    • Initiate: Assists and learns from those above them and fights for the Ecclesiarchy when required.
    • Priest: Tends to a temple and assists Preachers.
    • Preacher: Preaches in and around temples.
    • Cleric: Provides leadership and inspiration in the other Adepta.
    • Confessor: Watches for weakness, heresy and doubt in the population.
    • Exorcist: Roots out Chaos and heresy from Imperial citizens.
    • Bishop: Advises those in positions of power and leads Ecclesiarchy operations.
    • Zealot: Roots out Chaos and heresy in the Imperium.
    • Heirophant: Presides over a cathedral and advises the governor.
    • Redemptionist: Roots out Chaos and heresy in all corners of the Imperium.
