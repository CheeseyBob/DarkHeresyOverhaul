Traits - Dark Heresy Overhaul Index

Traits

Mechanically, bionics are Talents, but they cannot be aquired by spending XP - they are innate properties of the characters who have them.

Traits
Amorphous: You can pass through areas which are smaller than your Size would normally allow. You also have the Crawler Trait.
Armoured (N): Get a +N bonus to Armour Points.
Beastial: You don't need to make any Tests to survive in your natural habitat. Unless starving or desperate, you make a Willpower Test when frightened, startled or injured and flee if you fail.
Blind: Take a -30 penalty to any Test which would typically involve vision and automatically fail any Test which relies on it.
Burrower (N): Can Move through earth N meters.
Crawler: xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx has a slow move rate but is not affected by difficult terrain xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx
Daemonic: You are not affected by Psychic Phenomena, Bleeding Out, disease, poison and vacuum. You halve your Toughness against force weapons, psychic powers, holy attacks or other Daemonic characters.
Daemonic Presence (N): All non-daemonic characters within N meters take a -20 penalty to Willpower Tests.
Dark-Sight: You take no penalties due to Darkness.
Fear (N): When a character encounters you, they take a Fear Test.
Flyer (N): You can fly and your Agility Bonus counts as N while flying.
From Beyond: You are immune to Fear, Pinning, Insanity and anything which affects the mind.
Hoverer (N): You can hover a short distance above the ground, and can Move N meters while doing so.
Incoprorial: Get a +30 bonus to Conceal yourself and to Sneak. You can pass though solid objects, but cannot pass through Gellar fields. You can only harm or be harmed by Daemons, psychic powers, Warp creatures, other Incorporeal characters and force weapons.
Machine: You do not breathe and are immune to vacuum, cold and anything which affects the mind.
Programmed Behaviour: You are immune to all social Tests. If you are faced with a situation outside your programming, you must pass an Intelligence Test in order to act.
Phase: Secondary Action; Gain or remove the Incorporeal Trait.
Quadruped: Your base Movement is double your Agility Bonus.
Regeneration (N): You recover N Wounds each turn.
Sonar Sense: You can see up to 30m using sonar. Other characters can Notice this.
Stuff of Nightmares: You are not affected by Psychic Phenomena, Bleeding Out, disease, poison and vacuum. You are also immune to becoming Stunned and non-fatal Critical damage, unless caused by force weapons, psychic powers, holy attacks or other Daemonic characters.
Sturdy: Get a +20 bonus to oppose Knock Down and becoming Grappled.
Toxic: xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx deal damage to the attacker when wounded in melee xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx
Undying: You are unaffected by disease, poison and vacuum.
Unnatural Senses (N): You perceive your surroundings using means other than sight or sound, up to N meters.
Warp Instability: If you take any wounds, make a Willpower Test on your next turn. On failure, take 1 additional wound. On critical failure, you are cast back into the warp.
Warp Weapons: Your unarmed attacks ignore physical armour.
