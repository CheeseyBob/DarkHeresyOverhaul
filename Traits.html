Traits

Basic Physiology

▼▲

Arms (N): You have N arms.
Characters without this Trait are assumed to have 2 arms.
Beastial: You don't need to make any Tests to survive in your natural habitat. Unless starving or desperate, you make a Willpower Test when frightened, startled or injured and flee if you fail.
Crawler: You cannot Run, but are not affected by difficult terrain.
Dark-Sight: You take no penalties due to Darkness.
Fast: You get a +N bonus to your base movement.
Flight: You can fly and your Agility Bonus counts as N while flying.
Hover: You can hover a short distance above the ground, and can Move N meters while doing so.
Legs: You have N legs.
Characters without this Trait are assumed to have 2 legs.
Machine: You do not breathe and are immune to vacuum, cold and anything which affects the mind.
Size: You are affected by the appropriate bonuses/penalties for size N. Your Maximum Wounds is N.
Characters without this Trait are assumed to be Size 7 (human size).
Slow: You get a -N penalty to your base movement.
Sonar Sense: You can see up to N meters using sonar. Other characters can Notice this.
Unnatural Senses: You can perceive your surroundings up to N meters away using means other than sight or sound.

Physiology / Critical Damage

▼▲

Lost Hand: Take a -20 penalty to any Test which would typically involve 2 hands, or where you have strapped an item to your hand in place of holding it.
Lost Arm: Take a -20 penalty to any Test which would typically involve 2 hands.
Lost Foot: Take a -10 penalty to any Agility Test involving the use of legs. You become Prone on critical failures.
Lost Leg: Take a -10 penalty to any Agility Test involving the use of legs. Fall Prone on critical failures. Walking requires an Agility Test.
Lost Eye: Take a -10 penalty to any Test which would typically involve vision.
Blind: Take a -30 penalty to any Test which would typically involve vision and automatically fail any Test which relies on it.
Deaf: Take a -30 penalty to any Test which would typically involve hearing and automatically fail any Test which relies on it.
Mute: Take a -30 penalty to any Test which would typically involve speaking and automatically fail any Test which relies on it.

Daemons

▼▲

Daemonic: You are immune to disease, poison, vacuum, becoming Stunned or Bleeding Out, the effects of Psychic Phenomena and anything which affects the mind, such as Fear and Insanity. You halve your Toughness against force weapons, psychic powers, holy attacks or other Daemonic characters.
Daemonic characters should also have either the Dark-Sight or Unnatural Senses Trait.
Daemonic Presence: All non-daemonic characters within N meters take a -10 penalty to Willpower Tests.
Only Daemonic characters should have the Daemonic Presence Trait.
Incoprorial: Get a +30 bonus to Conceal yourself and to Sneak. You can pass though solid objects, but cannot pass through Gellar fields. You can only harm or be harmed by Daemons, psychic powers, Warp creatures, other Incorporeal characters and force weapons.
Phase: Secondary Action; Gain or remove the Incorporeal Trait.
Stuff of Nightmares: You are immune to all non-fatal effects of Critical damage, unless caused by force weapons, psychic powers, holy attacks or other Daemonic characters.
Warp Instability: If you take any wounds, make a Willpower Test on your next turn. On failure, take 1 additional wound. On critical failure, you are cast back into the warp.
Warp Weapons: Your unarmed attacks ignore physical armour.

Other

▼▲

Amorphous: You can pass through areas which are smaller than your Size would normally allow.
Amorphous characters should also have the Crawler Trait.
Armoured: Get a +N bonus to Armour Points.
Burrower: You can Move through earth N meters.
Fear Rating: When a character encounters you, they take a Fear Test.
Fear Ratings can be 1, 2, 3 or 4.
Fearless: You are immune to Fear Tests from Fear Rating N and below.
Programmed Behaviour: You are immune to all social Tests. If you are faced with a situation outside your programming, you must pass an Intelligence Test in order to act.
Regeneration: You recover N Wounds each turn.
Sturdy: Get a +20 bonus to oppose Knock Down and becoming Grappled.
Toxic: When a character wounds you with a melee attack, they become Poisoned.
Undying: You are unaffected by disease, poison and vacuum.
