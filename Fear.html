Fear - Dark Heresy Overhaul Index

Fear

When you are affected by Fear, you Resist (Mentally) to reduce the level of Fear by 1 plus 1 per Degree of Success. On critical failure, you instead increase the level of Fear by 1 and suffer a Mental Trauma. If the level of Fear is reduced below 0, you are unaffected.

Fear Level Effect
0 You take a -10 penalty to your next Test, as if you were Frightened.
1 You become Frightened.
2 You become Frightened(2) and cannot approach the source of your Fear until this is overcome/downgraded.
3 You become Frightened(3) and are frozen by terror until this is overcome/downgraded.
4 You become Frightened(4) and flee in panic until this is overcome/downgraded.
5 You become Frightened(5) and all unconscious until this is overcome/downgraded.
