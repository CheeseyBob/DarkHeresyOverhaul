Fear

When you are affected by Fear, you are subject to the effect of one of the Fear Levels - by default, this is Fear Level 4, but you Resist (Mentally) in order to change this. Apply a modifier to Resist (Mentally) based on the Fear Rating of the thing causing the Fear.

  • On success you reduce the Fear Level by 1 plus 1 per Degree of Success.
  • On failure, you take Insanity Points equal to 1d10-WB.
  • On critical failure, you increase the Fear Level by 1 and take Insanity Points equal to 1d10 plus the Fear Rating.
Fear Rating 1 2 3 4
Modifier +30 +20 +10 +0

Fear Level Effect
0 You take a -10 penalty to your next Test as if you were Frightened.
1 You become Frightened.
2 You become Frightened(2) and cannot approach the source of your Fear until this is overcome.
3 You become Frightened(3) and are frozen by terror until this is overcome.
4 You become Frightened(4) and flee in panic until this is overcome.
5 You become Frightened(5) and are reduced to a babbling mess until this is overcome.
Only one level of Frightened needs to be overcome to end the extra effects.

Mental Trauma

When you take a Trauma Test, you are subject to the effect of one of the Trauma Levels - by default, this is Trauma Level 4, but you Resist (Mentally) in order to change this. Apply a modifier to Resist (Mentally) as specified on the Insanity Track.

  • On success you reduce the Trauma Level by 1 plus 1 per Degree of Success.
  • On critical failure, you increase the Trauma Level by 1.

When you take a Trauma Test, you Resist (Mentally) to reduce the Trauma Level by 1 plus 1 per Degree of Success. On critical failure, you increase the Trauma Level by 1.

Trauma Level Effect
0 The character becomes withdrawn and quiet. [penalty to fellowship]
1 The character is constantly fearful and is extremely jumpy, seeing danger everywhere. [willpower penalty; perception bonus]
2 The character compulsively performs an action (such as fevered praying, frantically cleaning a weapon, etc.) and pays little attention to anything else. [penalty to Intelligence Perception and Fellowship]
3 The character reacts to the slightest pressure by becoming extremely agitated. [penalty to all tests out of combat after failing a willpower test / all tests in combat]
4 The character suffers vivid and extreme nightmares [fatigue after sleeping]
5 The character becomes completely traumatised and virtually unresponsive. They cannot initiate actions, but can be gently led.

* Talent - Resilient Mind - Get a +20 bonus to Trauma Tests.
