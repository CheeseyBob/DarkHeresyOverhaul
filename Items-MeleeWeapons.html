Bonus: This is the bonus you get to Weapon Skill when you Attack with the weapon.

Hits: This is the maximum number of hits by the weapon in one turn. If you Attack, you get up to one hit, whereas if you Attack Repeatedly, you can get a number of hits up to the weapon's Hits value.

Damage: All Melee Weapons add the wielder's Strength Bonus to the damage.

Availability: The base availability for Ranged Weapons is Abundant.

Throwing Melee Weapons

  • When you throw a Melee Weapon as an attack, you Attack (Ranged) using the Very Short range bracket for Thrown Weapons. Damage and other stats are the same as when you Attack (Melee) with the weapon.
