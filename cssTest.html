Fear

When you are affected by Fear, you are subject to the effect of one of the Fear Levels - by default, this is Fear Level 4, but you Resist (Mentally) in order to change this. Apply a modifier to Resist (Mentally) based on the Fear Rating of the thing causing the Fear.

  • On success you reduce the level of Fear by 1 plus 1 per Degree of Success.
  • On failure, you take Insanity Points equal to the Fear Rating.
  • On critical failure, you increase the level of Fear by 1 and take Insanity Points equal to 1d10 plus the Fear Rating.
