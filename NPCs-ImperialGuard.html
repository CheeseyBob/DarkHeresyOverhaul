Rank Descriptions

    • Conscript: Trains, performs menial duties and, if necessary, fights.
    • Guard: Fights on the front lines.
    • Specialist(Armsman): Uses specialised weapons.
    • Specialist(Comms): Operates comms devices.
    • Specialist(Demolitions): Uses explosives.
    • Specialist(Driver): Operates vehicles.
    • Specialist(Medic): Provides first aid.
    • Sergeant: Ensures orders are relayed to and carried out by their squad.
    • Veteran: Fights in units that can be relied upon to hold their ground.
    • Assault Veteran: Fights in units that can be relied upon to take ground.
    • Lieutenant: Leads their unit, either of Veterans or of several squads led by Sergeants.
    • Scout: Locates and observes enemy forces using stealth.
    • Shock Trooper: Fights in high-stakes maneuvers such as storming bunkers.
    • Captain: Commands large formations and battle groups.
    • Marksman: Provides long-range fire support, digging in and picking off the enemy.
    • Storm Trooper: Fights in high-stakes special operations.
    • Commander: Controls the flow of battle from planning to execution.
    • Sniper: Provides long-range fire support, killing key targets then melting away from view.
