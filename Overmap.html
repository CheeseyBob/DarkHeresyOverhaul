Overmap

*** travel 1 hex per hour
*** Test Navigation in difficult terrain - on a failure, take an extra hour; on a critical failure, stray off course, stumble upon danger
*** Stray off course - end up in a hex left/right of your intended path.
*** FINDING LOCATIONS - unfamiliar locations are found using encounter checks.
- roll 1d10 to determine if/which previously encountered location is encountered. If the roll is 10 or is over the number of previously encountered locations in the area, then a new location is generated and encountered. Otherwise count down the list of existing locations by the rolled number to determine the location encountered.

*** ENCOUNTER CHECKS
- If the characters are stationary, ignore results for locations
- 50% location; 50% other encounter?
- small chance of rolling twice and combining results?
- roll spot distances & surprise
- roll disposition scale
** Combining multiple results can be done in various ways - for example, a camp of mutants plus a heretek cabal could be taken to mean either the mutants are working with the hereteks, or are in confict with the hereteks, or even that the hereteks are themselves mutants.
