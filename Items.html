Aquiring Items - Purchase and Requisition

Characters can aquire items and services either through payment and barter, or by using their influence and authority to requisition what is needed. Requisition is affected by:

  • The justification you give.
  • Whether you reveal you are working for the Inquisition.
  • The NPC's disposition towards you.

Identifying Items

  • Recall Common/Forbidden/Scholastic Lore to determine how much the character knows.
  • Scrutinise to determine how much the character can discover through observation.
  • Contemplate to determine how much the character can piece together through deduction.

Crafting

  • Materials and designs: all creations require materials; tech items also require designs.
  • Number of steps to craft: 1-5 for simple items; 5-10 for more complicated items; 10+ for very complicated items.(??)
  • Difficulty: use the availablity bonus/penalty.
