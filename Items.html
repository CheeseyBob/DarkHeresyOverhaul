Ranged Weapons

*** Prices need rethinking ***

Weapon Jams: Whenever you fire a ranged weapon, a roll of 97-100 is an automatic miss and indicates your weapon has Jammed. For Extended Fire, this happens on a roll of 93-100.

Range: Use the table to determine the bonus you get to hit when shooting. Use the highest bonus for which the target is within the corresponding distance (for example, you always get a +30 bonus to a target 3m away). Firing at a target beyond the maximum range is ineffective.

RoF (Rate of Fire): This is the number of shots fired when making an attack with the weapon, which is also the maximum number of hits for that attack. The first number is the standard rate of fire; the second number is the increased rate of fire, used for Extended Fire actions.

Ammo: Reloading a weapon takes a Full Action. You can make attacks if you don't have enough ammo loaded - just use the actual number of shots fired for the maximum number of hits.

Armour

*** These prices should be OK ***

Armour privides AP when worn. Broadly speaking:
  • Primitive armour is AP 1-2.
  • Flak armour is AP 2-3 with a bonus against Explosive damage.
  • Mesh armour is AP 3-4 with a bonus against Energy damage.
  • Carapace armour is AP 4-5 with a bonus against Impact damage.
  • Power armour is AP 7-8.
