Bionics

Mechanically, bionics are Talents, but they cannot be aquired by spending XP. Rather, they are purchased as if they are Items and must be installed by a capable chirurgeon in a proper medical facility (see Items for availability of medical care). The difficulty of installing a bionic is the modifier for its availability. A failure inflicts 1d10 Rending damage on the character, ignoring Armour and Toughness.
