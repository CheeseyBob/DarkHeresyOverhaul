Skills

Skills give a bonus to the action of the same name when taken by the character with the Skill. Some skills are Basic, which means all characters are assumed to have the skill at +0.
***** Advancements - spend XP after attempting the action you are improving. Spend a Fate Point to attempt an Advanced Action you don't have the skill for in order to get the opportunity to purchase it with XP. Also can spend XP by spending time training.
***** Fate Points: If you choose not to burn a Fate Point to save your character from dying, your next character starts with an extra Fate Point.
