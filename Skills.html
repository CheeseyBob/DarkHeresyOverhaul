Skills

Skills are a particular kind of Aspect which can be permenantly attached to characters. They cannot be Overcome and their only effect is to give a bonus to the action of the same name, when taken by the character with the Skill. Skills cannot give bonuses to Attack actions.
***** Basic vs Advanced? Basic = bonus only; Advanced = allows the skill use
