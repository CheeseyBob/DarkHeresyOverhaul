Throwing Melee Weapons

When you throw a Melee Weapon as an attack, simply treat the item as a Thrown Weapon - use the damage and special rules (if doing so makes sense) on the item card.

Throwing Other Items

When you throw another item as an attack, treat it as an Improvised Melee Weapon of the appropriate size.
