Fate Points

Fate points are a meta-game resource which each player has. The GM does not get Fate Points. You start with 3 and get 3 more whenever your character dies. When you choose a new character, all Fate Points (spent and unspent) are moved over to your new character.

Spending Fate Points

You can spend a Fate Point to reroll any Test and use your preferred result. You get spent Fate Points back at the start of each session.

Burning Fate Points

You can also burn a Fate Point to either save your character from an action which would have killed them, or to bench your character (at an appropriate time) and roll a new one. You do not get back burnt Fate Points, but you can burn spent Fate Points.

Bonus Fate Points

You can earn bonus Fate Points if you have your character take a risky action which fits the character (in other words, when you maintain roleplaying to your detriment).
