Rank Descriptions

    • Technographer: Identifies and catalogues technology and assists with repairs.
    • Mech-Wright: Conducts heavy repairs and tends dangerous manufactorum processes.
    • Electro-Priest: Learns the animating principals of machine spirits and how they can be appeased.
    • Engine-Seer: Tends complicated machines, finding faults and removing errors from all manner of technology.
    • Tech-Priest: Seeks out the many forms of the Omnissiah to add more knowledge to the data crypts of the Adeptus Mechanicus.
    • Technomancer: Extract information that may be of use to the Machine Cult.
    • Mech-Deacon: Deals with those ignorant of the Omnissiah, either by providing aid or by delivering punishments.
    • Cyber-Seer: Delves into forbidden and occult matters in their quest for knowledge.
    • Omniprophet: Spreads the ways of the Cult Mechanicus to all manner of Imperial subjects and also watches for items or matters of interest.
    • Magos: Serves the Adeptus Mechanicus with their enormous accumulated knowledge.
    • Magos Errant: Goes forth to explore new frontiers, worlds and technologies.
