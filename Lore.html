Lore

There are three uses for the lore skills:

  • Get Info to learn Characteristics, Personality, Traits, Talents, etc. about a character, item or something else. You can do this automatically when you first encounter something, or when you take the time to do research.
  • Get Clues to obtain Clues about something for an investigation. You can do this when you take the time to do research.
  • Assist to give a bonus to another Action by creating an Aspect. You can do this with a brief moment of thought after you have successfully identified the thing in question.

Note that research can only be done with Scholastic Lore or Forbidden Lore and requires access to the appropriate facilities.
