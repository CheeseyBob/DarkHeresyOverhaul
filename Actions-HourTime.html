Hour-Time Actions - Dark Heresy Overhaul Index

Hour-Time Actions

Any number of turn-time Actions can be done in one hour.


*** Critical Failure = roll over double the number to beat, or roll 00.
Basic Non-Combat Actions
Action Type Description Critical Failure
Heal Wounds Overcome Test Intelligence to remove Wounds from the target. The target takes an additional Wound.
Travel Overcome Travel for 1 hour through terrain which is simple to navigate. -
Navigate Overcome Test Intelligence to Travel through terrain which is hard to navigate. Stray off course or into harm's way.
Identify Item Overcome Test Intelligence to figure out what an item is and does, how to use it and what its stats are. The item is misidentified in some key way.
Repair Item Overcome Test Intelligence to repair a faulty or damaged item. The item becomes further damaged.
