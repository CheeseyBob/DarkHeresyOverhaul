Armour Points (AP): When you take a hit, you reduce the damage by the AP of the armour you are wearing (and your Toughness Bonus).

Fitting

Armour items are assumed to fit size 7 characters (humans). Where this is not the case, the item should have a special rule (and the item size changed accordingly).

GM Guide For Item Creation

For Armour, apply modifiers from the table below to a base availability of +1 (Common). Modifiers should also be applied for special rules the item has.

Item size doesn't matter much for armour, so does not affect the availability.
